Who I am

Welcome

My name is Samuel Mwai Kimani,

I’m a young lawyer, a community organizer, I have worked with the youths, organized tournament and volunteered in cleaning our environment. Going into politics wasn’t in the plan, but after years of the same representation, we have to ask, who have Njoro been changing for?
Everyday gets harder for working families like mine to get by, no jobs, poor roads and lack of water in almost every household.It is clear that these changes haven’t been for us, and we deserve a champion, it’s time to fight for a Njoro that families can be proud of, that’s why I’m running for a parliamentary position.
This race is about people versus money, we’ve got the people and they’ve got money. It time we acknowledge that not all aspirants for this position care about us, they profit on our struggles, they don’t live here, don’t take their children to our schools, don’t drink our water or walk on our roads in a rainy night, and so, cannot possibly represent us!
